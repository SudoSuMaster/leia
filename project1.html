Toronto Cupcake

2022

Project Overview

During my first year in CMD, I redesigned the website for Toronto Cupcake, a cupcake shop in Toronto. Through a heuristic analysis, I identified non-user-friendly elements on the website and improved them through a redesign to enhance usability. My objective was to provide a better user experience for visitors to the website, enabling them to easily order cupcakes and find information about the assortment and the shop.
