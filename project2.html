Project Overview

One project I'm proud of is when I recreated 'The Truman Show' through video editing. I added my own unique style to it, focusing on making it visually interesting. By using smooth transitions and eye-catching visuals, I aimed to make Truman's journey feel more real and captivating. It was a fun challenge that allowed me to tell a story in a creative way that people could really connect with.
